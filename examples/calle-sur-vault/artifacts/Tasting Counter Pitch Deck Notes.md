- Pricing precedent: West Coast omakase = $80–$150/person (our $95–$120 is conservative)

**Positioning:**
- "Calle Sur's Inner Circle" (internal brand)

**Customer profile:**
- Couples, anniversaries, food obsessives, affluent tourists

## Slide 3: Revenue Model

**Capacity & Frequency:**
- 8 seats, 2 potential seatings/night (90–120 min service = feasible)
- Target: 3 nights/week initially (Fri/Sat/Sun), 4 nights/week by year 2
- Full capacity: 48 covers/week (year 1 conservative estimate)

**Pricing:**
- Tasting menu: $95/person (food only)
- Wine pours (optional): $45–$60 add-on, ~70% attachment rate
- Blended check average: $125/person (food + wine)

**Weekly Revenue (Year 1, 3 nights/week, 85% occupancy):**
- 40 covers/week × $125 avg = **$5,000/week**
- 52 weeks = **$260K annual revenue**

**Margin:**
- Food cost: ~$32/cover (25% of $125 blended)
- Labor (dedicated): ~$18/cover (additional payroll)
- Gross margin: ~$75/cover = **60% blended**
- Weekly margin: $3,000 (at full capacity)
