# Launch Readiness

This is the active checklist for the Calle Sur fermented hot sauce line. It
should be the best match when an agent asks about launch status, current blockers,
or next actions for the hot sauce project.

## Current State

- Recipe v3 is locked for Classic Red.
- Habanero Amber remains in internal testing.
- Aji Limo Verde depends on spring and summer pepper availability.
- Co-packer shortlist is down to two viable partners.
- Label language still needs regulatory review before the first commercial run.

## Open Launch Blockers

1. Confirm co-packer fermentation room can hold 20 C within a two-degree band.
2. Reconcile label ingredient order after final pH and salt verification.
3. Decide whether first retail cases ship direct-to-consumer only or include two
   regional specialty accounts.
4. Build a shelf-stable sample pack for buyers without overcommitting inventory.
5. Confirm first batch freight assumptions against [[Email - Distributor Quote Hot Sauce Co-Pack]].
