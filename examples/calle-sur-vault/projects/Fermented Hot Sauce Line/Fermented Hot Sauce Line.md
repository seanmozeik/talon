# Fermented Hot Sauce Line

**Target launch:** June 2026. Retail hot sauce bottled under Calle Sur label. 3-SKU line: Classic Red, Habanero Amber, Aji Limo Verde. Margin: 65–70% (COGS ~$1.20/bottle, retail $4.50). Co-packer partnership to scale production beyond kitchen capacity.

**Status:** Recipe locked (v3). Co-packer vetting in progress. Target: sign contract by May 15, first batch mid-June.

## Vision

Hot sauce born from Calle Sur kitchen fermentation practice. Authentic lacto-fermentation (no vinegar shortcuts). Positioning: craft, small-batch, seasonal (ingredients rotate by sourcing window). Premium retail price justified by technique, not volume.

Distribution: direct-to-consumer (website, mail order), regional food retailers (5–10 accounts target year 1), mail-order specialty clubs.

## Product Line

**Classic Red** (flagship)
- 60% red Fresno, 25% habanero, 15% aji limo
- 2.5% salt (by weight)
- 14-day ferment at 20°C
- Heat level: 5/10 (medium)
- Yield: 500 ml bottles, ~200 bottles/week at full production

See [[Recipe v3]] for full spec.

**Habanero Amber** (variant, TBD)
- Higher habanero ratio, slower ferment (21 days)
- Target: 7/10 heat level
- Testing phase: internal batches only, launch delayed to August if needed

**Aji Limo Verde** (seasonal spring/summer)
- Green aji limo (sourced from Salt Marsh or Costera)
- 2.5% salt, 10-day ferment
- Fresh, bright, less heat (4/10)
- Seasonal only (April–September availability)

## Fermentation Foundation

All three SKUs are **true lacto-fermentation:** salt + peppers + time, no added vinegar. The fermentation acidity is the acid driver. 2.5% salt by weight is the consistency across all recipes (tested, consistent, food-safe).

Wild ferments (ambient microbes) are managed through salt concentration and controlled temperature. Co-packer must maintain 18–22°C fermentation room (non-negotiable for consistency).

See [[Lacto-Fermentation]] and [[Koji and Aspergillus]] for fermentation theory & safety protocols.

## Co-Packer Partnership

Need facility with:
- 100+ liter fermentation capacity
- 20°C ± 2°C controlled environment
- Bottling line (minimal: capsule filler, labeler, capper)
- Regulatory compliance (FDA, state canning/fermentation permits)
- Scale target: 2,400 bottles/month (200/week per SKU)

## Financial Model

**COGS per 500 ml bottle:**
- Peppers: $0.45
- Salt, spices: $0.15
- Fermentation labor (co-packer): $0.20
- Bottling, label, shipping to us: $0.40
- **Total COGS: $1.20**

**Retail price: $4.50** (direct-to-consumer)  
**Wholesale price: $2.70** (retailers, 40% margin to distributor)

Gross margin at retail: 73% ($4.50 − $1.20).

Year 1 target: 5,000 bottles (~$22.5K revenue at retail mix). Breakeven: ~3,000 bottles.

## Sourcing

**Peppers:**
- Red Fresno: Costera Fish seasonal availability (April–October)
- Habanero: cultivated supplier (consistent year-round)
- Aji limo: Costera Fish or import (Chile/Peru sourcing)

Frozen or fresh? Frozen peppers work (tested in R&D phase), saves logistics, allows off-season production. Decision: freeze Fresno/aji limo fresh, habanero can be fresh or frozen.

## Logistics & Fulfillment

- **Website:** Shopify store live May 15 (pending final recipe approval)
- **Shipping:** USPS Priority Mail flat-rate boxes (fits 6 bottles comfortably)
- **Packaging:** Custom cardboard shipper, branded tissue, thank-you card
- **Cost per order:** ~$3.50 (packaging + label + postage for 2-bottle minimum order)

Pre-orders open May 1; first shipments July 1 (post-launch batch).

## Regulatory

- **FDA/State:** Bottled hot sauce requires process authority certification (pH, heat treatment) or acidification plan. Lacto-fermentation achieves pH <4.0 naturally (tested), so no additional acidification needed. Co-packer must have food safety plan on file.
- **Label:** Nutrition facts, ingredient list, allergen statement. Designing now; approval 2 weeks.
- **Permits:** Food establishment license (Calle Sur covers production oversight), but co-packer needs their own license.
