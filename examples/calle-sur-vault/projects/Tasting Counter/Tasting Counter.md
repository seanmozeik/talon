# Tasting Counter

**Goal:** 8-seat tasting counter inside Calle Sur (separate ticketed experience). **Timeline:** Fall 2026 soft opening. **Status:** Discovery phase. Budget and lease constraints need clarity.

## Concept

Counter seats 8 maximum. Separate entrance from main dining room (or velvet rope divide). Dedicated prep station visible to guests. 90-minute or 2-hour omakase-style seated experience, hosted by Lúcia or myself. Higher check average than main dining (~$95–$120 per person). Margin targets 50%+.

Idea born from seeing omakase concepts on West Coast (high margin, high engagement). Calle Sur's kitchen is technically small, but Lúcia's precision plating scales. Risk: kitchen workflow during service (can't lose main dining experience).

## Market & Positioning

- 8-seat tasting is rare in region; positioning as "invitation-first, walk-ins if space" creates scarcity.
- Demographic: couples, anniversaries, food obsessives, tourists with budget.
- Price point: $95–$120/person + wine pours ($40–$60 more). Revenue per night: $1200–$1280 (full house, 2 seatings if feasible).
- Sustainability: need 50 covers/week off main dining to justify build-out. 8 seats × 2 seatings × 3 nights = 48 covers realistic first year.

## Constraints

### Space

Main dining room is 40 seats. Kitchen is galley-style, ~120 sqft. Adding counter seats 8–10 in front of kitchen requires:
- Reconfiguring main bar (move cocktail station west)
- Exhaust upgrades (salamander + main vent load increases)
- ADA compliance for ingress/egress

See [[Layout Sketches]] for 3 conceptual approaches.

### Lease & Landlord

Calle Sur is under a 3-year lease starting 2025-01-01. Lease Notes say no major kitchen modifications without written approval. Need landlord sign-off on exhaust work. See [[Lease Notes 2026]] (private) for full terms. Estimated landlord approval window: 4–6 weeks.

### Budget

Rough build-out estimate:
- Counter + seating: $8K (custom 12 ft teak counter, 8 × stools)
- Prep station + plumbing: $6K
- Exhaust + HVAC mods: $10K–$15K
- FF&E (plates, glassware, linens): $3K

**Total: $27K–$32K.** Profitability breakeven: ~3 months (at 50% occupancy). Payback: 6 months. Viable if revenue holds.

### Staffing

- **Service:** Renata or hire dedicated tasting counter host (salary ~$35K/yr).
- **Kitchen:** Lúcia as primary cook (impacts plating on main line during service; risk).
- **Dishpit:** Need 1 additional staffer during tasting seatings (currently Jonas solo during dinner service).

Additional payroll: ~$18K/yr.
