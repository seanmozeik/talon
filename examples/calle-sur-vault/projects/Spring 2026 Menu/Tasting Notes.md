# Tasting Notes — April 18, 2026

Team tasting: myself, Lúcia, David, Renata. Intention: validate spring menu before May 5 debut. Result: solid backbone. Two dishes needed minor technique tweaks; lamb neck is money.

## Charred Spring Onion

**First impression:** Muddy. Char flavor present but whispered. Whey cream was too neutral, too heavy. Spring onion itself was perfect—buttery, just-tender, great char crust.

**Feedback:**
- David: "Needs acid. It's pretty but not alive."
- Lúcia: "Whey is killing it. We need to reduce it more, brighten the oil."
- Renata: "Could use a textural element—something crispy."

**Revision:** Reduced whey further (target 1:1 reduction ratio instead of 1.5:1). Bumped olive oil from 0.5 tsp to 0.75 tsp. Swapped cultivated garlic shoots for wild-foraged (sharper, greener).

**Retasted April 22 (staff meal):** Lúcia confirmed it now pops. Worth serving.

See [[Dish - Charred Spring Onion]] for final spec.

## Fava and Whey

**First impression:** Alive. Fermentation acidity singing. Texture is velvety, tastes like spring.

**Feedback:**
- David: "Pairs beautifully with Loire Sauv Blanc or orange wine. The ferment acidity matches white wine acidity. I'm recommending Sancerre, 2023 vintage."
- Lúcia: "Perfect. Tendrils are the key—they make it."
- Renata: "Price point is right. Easy to repeat."

**No changes needed.** Dish is locked. See [[Dish - Fava and Whey]] for spec.

> [!info] Wine Notes
> David's recommendation: **Sauvignon Blanc** (Loire, Sancerre or similar) or **Skin-contact white** (natural, funky). Ferment + wine acidity = clean, alive drinking.

## Lamb Neck

**First impression:** Tender, luxe, shows craft. Sauce is glossy and restrained. Meat had real bite, not mushy.

**Feedback:**
- David: "This is the anchor. Wine pairing is wide open—pair it with a rustic red, 2022 vintage Côtes du Rhône or Spanish field blend."
- Lúcia: "Braise technique is perfect. Not overcooked. Renato's lamb quality is showing."
- Myself: "Plate cost is high but margin holds. We can run this for 40 covers weekly."
- Renata: "Staff love it. This sells the experience."

**No changes.** Dish is locked. See [[Dish - Lamb Neck]] for spec.
